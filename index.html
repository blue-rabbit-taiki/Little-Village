    Little Village

    ~Welcome to our organic field, the natural garden in the country side~

  • There are several kinds of flowers, fruits, vegetables in our field all year round..

  • Soil is important..

    In our region, most of the soil included is called “Kanto Loam”.

    Kanto Loam is made of volcanic ash and it is so high nutritional value.

    We grow crops to use this soil and they will be the great product.

  • And water..

    We just water the crops once at a week but it's not enough..

    Rain helps our agri-life.

    Crops are absorbing water and freshly grown up..

  • Bright Sun light will promote photosynthesis..

    The combination of climate and soil condition will make great crops..

  • Finally,we can create the ideal field.

    The fresh crops are growing day by day.

    Our farm is gradually designed as well..
